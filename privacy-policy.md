# Privacy Policy for TackTracker Sailing

**Last updated: March 24, 2026**

This privacy policy describes how TackTracker Sailing ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information.

---

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email address** — used to create and authenticate your account. Not displayed publicly.
- **Display name** — a username you choose, visible to other sailors in your regatta.

### Location Data
- **Precise GPS location** — collected during active races and sandbox sessions to track your position on the course, detect mark roundings, and record your GPS trail.
- Location is collected while the app is in use and, during active races, in the background so your track is not interrupted when the screen locks.

### Race Data
- **Boat name, sail number, and boat class** — entered by you when registering for a race.
- **Race performance data** — GPS track, elapsed times, and split times recorded during races.

---

## How We Use Your Information

- To authenticate you and manage your account
- To track your position on the race course during active races
- To share your position and race data with other sailors in your regatta for post-race analytics
- To display race results and performance history to you

---

## How We Share Your Information

- **Other sailors in your regatta** — your boat name, sail number, and race performance data are visible to other registered participants in the same race.
- **We do not sell your data** to any third parties.
- **We do not share your data** with advertisers or marketing companies.

---

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (supabase.com). Location data is only uploaded during active race sessions.

---

## Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

---

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
